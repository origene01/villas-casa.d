Villa

This Dominican Republic Resort Villa comes complete with every luxury amenity, including full time Housekeeping Staff, a private pool and outdoor heated Jacuzzi.

Located in the southeastern coast of the Dominican Republic, Casa de Campo offers 15,000 acres of tropical Caribbean paradise. It is perfect for Weddings, Honeymoons and family vacations. The 5 golf courses are spectacular. They include "Teeth of the Dog", "Dye Fore" and The Romana Country Club that Robb Report lists and one of the most exclusive in the world.

The Resort is a lush Caribbean retreat of unsurpassed elegance and breathtaking beauty where action, relaxation and pampering service combine for an exceptional leisure experience. Here you will find a "Quality of Life" unavailable anywhere in the western hemisphere. The atmosphere is safe, quiet and full of the most rewarding pleasures, dining and activities found anywhere in the Caribbean.

Casa de Campo Marine

As a port destination, the Casa de Campo Marina is like no other in the Caribbean! The centerpiece of the Marina is the promenade, a winding street running through the original breakwater and presents a striking combination of the lushness of the Caribbean countryside and the more classic Italian gardens of gazebos, pergolas and fountains. Fine restaurants and shops include Bang & Olufsen, Bleu Marine, Dedon and Hatteras Yachts.

Casa de Campo Marina has become a perfect home port for the owners of large yachts who desire to travel around the Caribbean. The Marina has become a high-end Caribbean destination, much like St. Barts, considered by many to be the most glamorous and exclusive island in the Caribbean. It provides the wealthy yacht owner a complete Port o' Call offering the most upscale shops, fine dining and services including an international airport (LRM)

AMENITIES

RESIDENCE - Lending gracious tropical Caribbean style, this beautifully appointed Villa residence provides 6,000 square feet of spacious living with 5 bedrooms and 5-1/2 baths plus full maid's quarters. Constructed in 2004 and impeccably maintained, this distinctive Private Caribbean Villa showcases mahogany French doors, Travertine marble floors and is gracefully furnished for aesthetics and comfort. The open floor plan, Living/Dining Room and grand sitting areas lead to the expansive terrace and pool area.

ACTIVITIES

Welcome to the Casa de Campo Resort Experience. The resort offers a unique variety of different facilities. There are 5 Championship Golf Courses by noted course designer Pete Dye, A World Class Marina and Tennis Facilities, Private Beach, Horse Ranch, Polo fields, A Championship Level Shooting Center, Cygalle Healing Spa and many other attractions. Relax in the feeling of being far away from the modern world in a simple, relaxing, yet elegant setting.
